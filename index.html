How to Play

  1. Press the A-W-S-D keys to move your ship and avoid the asteroid.
  2. Use your cursor to fire your gun and destroy asteroids Keep in mind larger asteroids will take more hits to destroy!
  3. Rack up points by destroying asteroids and watch your health!
  4. Press space bar to use special power. Watch your Mana Bar!
  5. Power-Ups refill your mana by 20.
  6. To begin, click the start button! Don't die LOSER!
